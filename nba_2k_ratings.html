How the OVR was calculated
Every attribute (outside shooting, ball handling, passing, rebounding, interior defence, perimeter defence, athleticism, IQ) was scored 40–99 based on the per-game stats — then weighted by position importance to produce the final OVR. Physical size matters too: J.A's 6'4/250 frame gives him a head start on interior attributes that smaller players can't match regardless of skill. E.S's OVR is boosted by the fact that 2K weights winning and impact stats — his +3.67 plus/minus and 72.7% win rate are real upward pushes.
