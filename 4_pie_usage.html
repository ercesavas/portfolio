PIE formula (open-shot FGM/FGA only)

fgm = made_1pt + made_2pt  |  fga = att_1pt + att_2pt
player_impact = pts + fgm − fga + rbs_def + (rbs_off × 0.5) + ass + stl + (blk × 0.5) − to
PIE = player_impact / sum of all players' impact in that game

Contested shots excluded for consistency — they were only tracked from March 2025 onwards so including them would bias early-season games.

Usage rate (adjusted — no minutes played)

USG% = (fga + to) / team(fga + to)
Since every player plays every minute, this measures each player's share of their team's possession-ending actions per game.
