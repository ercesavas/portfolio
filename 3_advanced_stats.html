Advanced Stats

eFG% · ORB% · DRB% · TRB% · EFF/g · PIE median — all group-relative
What each stat means

eFG% — adjusts shooting % for 2pt shots worth more. (FGM + 0.5×2ptMade) / FGA
ORB% — of all available offensive rebounds, what % you grabbed.
DRB% — same for defensive rebounds.
TRB% — combined rebounding rate.
EFF/g — PTS + REB + AST + STL + BLK − TOV − missed shots. Per game.
PIE median — your typical share of total game impact. Fair share in 4-player game = 0.25.
